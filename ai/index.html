The standard here

1

Real workflow first

The question is always where this fits into a real process, team, decision, or output.

2

Claims need friction

If a claim sounds too smooth, it probably ignored cost, maintenance, ambiguity, or human review.

3

Tools are not strategy

A stack of tools does not replace thinking. Bad structure with AI is still bad structure.

4

Clarity beats spectacle

The goal is not to look advanced. The goal is to create actual leverage and reduce wasted motion.
